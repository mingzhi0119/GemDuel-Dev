- Strongest families in this pass are card backs, gem panels, and shell backgrounds. Moonlit Jade, Ember Forge, Pearl Opaline, and Neon Noir each produced clear material identities without readable text in the reviewed contact sheets.

- Pearl Opaline is intentionally bright; inspect contrast behind React-rendered labels before adopting it for a full shell or card back.

- One-pixel card-back corrections are low-risk dimensional normalizations, but inspect border symmetry before final integration.
- Shell backgrounds were upscaled from 16:9 sources; acceptable for candidate review, but final production picks may benefit from a native 3840x2160 regeneration.
