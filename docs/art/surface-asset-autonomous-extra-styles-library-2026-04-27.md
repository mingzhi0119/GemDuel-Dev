- Topbar and PlayerZone outputs remain the highest-risk slots because Image Gen often returns concept-art aspect ratios that must be cropped into very wide UI bands.

- Shell backgrounds were upscaled from 16:9 generated sources; acceptable for candidate review, but final production picks may benefit from native 3840x2160 regeneration.
- Bright Solar Enamel surfaces need contrast review with React labels and controls before any promotion.
