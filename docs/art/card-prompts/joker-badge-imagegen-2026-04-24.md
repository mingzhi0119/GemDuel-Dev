Use case: stylized-concept

Reference direction: a premium circular gold-framed badge containing a faceted rainbow diamond/prism. Regenerate it with cleaner internal design.

Greatly enlarge the central rainbow diamond so the diamond occupies about 80% of the inner circular field. Change the inner badge background to a warm ivory parchment/ivory enamel backing, not dark smoke. Keep the outer gold circular frame ornate and polished.

Composition: centered square icon, circular badge silhouette, gold rim, ivory inner disk, one large central faceted rainbow diamond/prism nearly filling the inner circle but not touching the frame. The diamond should be the dominant subject and readable at small card size.

Style: high-end fantasy board-game asset, crisp transparent gemstone facets, restrained luxury, subtle natural prism sparkle only on the gem itself, no clutter.

Background outside badge: perfectly flat solid `#ff00ff` magenta chroma-key background for later removal. No shadows on background, no gradient, no floor, no texture.

Avoid: colored rays around the gemstone, five-color lines, pie chart, separated small gems, text, numbers, letters, playing-card suits, jester face, crown-only emblem, cost icons, watermark, modern flat UI.
