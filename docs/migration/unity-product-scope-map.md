## Current Scope Conclusion

No Electron player-facing surface is yet user-approved for exclusion. Full migration completion is
therefore blocked until LAN, online, Visual Lab, full live gameplay replay coverage, Joker
release/edge coverage, reserved-buy release/edge coverage, reserve deck oracle coverage, remaining
reserve-cancel edge fixtures, discard-reserved, draft reroll, remaining privilege oracle/rejection
fixtures, remaining peek/modal oracle/release fixtures, invalid-action recovery, and arbitrary Local
PvP are either implemented with parity evidence or explicitly excluded by the user. LAN opponent
visibility preferences now have Unity setting and persistence evidence, but cannot be applied to
real LAN gameplay until the LAN route exists.
